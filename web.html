About RJ Eatery

Rj Eatery is a restaurant and delivery service in the city of Kathmandu. We are a family owned business which was established in 2078 B.S. with the purpose of providing quality food at affordable prices. We are here to serve you. We deliver the best quality food to your doorstep, in your mentioned time. We provide you with a wide range of drinks and desserts. we also organize parties and events. We also have sub restaurants in Kathmandu and other major cities in Nepal. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us from our contact page. If you come with your own food, we will make sure that it is fresh and delicious. If you come with your family or friends, we will make sure that they are satisfied with our service.

We also take online payments via credit cards and debit cards and our online partners Esewa, Khalti and IME pay.
